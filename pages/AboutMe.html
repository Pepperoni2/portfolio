Education And Experience

2017-2022

I pursued my studies in Electronics and Computer Science at the Higher Technical College of Moedling in Lower Austria.
During this time, I built a foundation in several programming languages, including C, C++, and C#. I also learned to create websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
For my diploma thesis, I developed both the front-end and back-end of a dynamic ticket-shop website. This experience ignited my passion for full-stack web development and inspired my interest in creative technology solutions.

2023-2024

A few months after graduating, I began working as a Full-stack Developer at a small company in Vienna, where I spent about a year and a half.
There, I gained hands-on experience and refined my programming skills while also picking up new languages and frameworks, such as Delphi, Laravel, and VueJS.
This role provided valuable insight into the intricacies of developing complex programs and web applications, deepening my understanding of both front-end and back-end development.

2024-Present

I am currently studying Creative Computing at the University of Applied Sciences in St. Pölten.
The program centers on game development, design, and web development—along with many other exciting areas—making it an ideal fit as I work toward a career in these fields.
Ultimately, I hope to pursue opportunities in this industry, possibly as a freelancer.
